2011 / 2012 NATIONAL ICT QUIZ – Tagged national IT-QUEST 2011

In 2011, an exhilarating and educational event took place in Nigeria — the National ICT Quiz for secondary schools. This prestigious quiz was initiated by Davoi Foundation, with support from the following

◼ Federal ministry of education

◼ National Information Technology Development Agency (NITDA)

◼ State Ministries of Education

◼ FCT Secondary Education Board

◼ FCT Universal Basic Education board

◼ Education Resources Centre (ERC)

◼ AkoA Integrated Services

◼ Dattel Microsystems Ltd

◼ Cinfores Ltd

Focusing on Information and Communication Technology (ICT), the quiz aimed to test the knowledge and skills of participating students. Throughout the event, students from various secondary schools engaged in a spirited competition, answering a range of challenging ICT questions based on the Federal Ministry of Education (Nigerian Educational Research and Development Council) curriculum. The atmosphere was charged with excitement as the young minds shared ideas, showcasing their expertise and passion for technology. The quiz progressed through multiple rounds, ultimately leading to the highly anticipated finals held at International conference centre, Abuja, Nigeria.

As the competition reached its climax, the finalists battled it out for the top positions, vying for prestigious prizes and trophies. The anticipation was palpable as the students demonstrated their abilities, with their teachers providing unwavering support and encouragement throughout the event. To ensure the participants' comfort and convenience, accommodations were arranged for both the students and their teachers during their stay in Abuja. This gesture not only facilitated their focus on the quiz but also fostered an environment of camaraderie and shared experiences among the participants.

The National Quiz for secondary schools in Nigeria in 2011 was a remarkable event that combined the thrill of competition with the promotion of ICT knowledge among young students. It served as a platform for intellectual growth, enabling students to showcase their talents, culture and learn from one another.
